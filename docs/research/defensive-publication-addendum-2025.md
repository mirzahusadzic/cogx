## Advanced Systems Innovations (#28-46)

**Published**: October 27, 2025 - November 19, 2025

### 28. Lattice Algebra System

**Innovation**: Boolean query operations across overlays with ASCII syntax.

**Key Claims**:
- Boolean operators: Meet (∧), Union (∪), Intersection (∩), Difference (\), Complement (¬)
- ASCII-only syntax: `+`, `-`, `&`, `|`, `~`, `->`
- Compositional query language across all overlays
- Algebraic properties: associativity, commutativity, distributivity
